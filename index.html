Know Your Akan Name!!!

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. In Ghana, children are given their
first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week
they were born. Learn more about the Akan Names.
Here are Ghanian day names.

    Male

  • Monday-Kwadwo
  • Tuesday-Kwabena
  • Wednesday-Kwahu
  • Thursday-Yaw
  • Friday-Kofi
  • Saturday-Kwame
  • Sunday-Kwasi

    Female

  • Monday-Adowa
  • Tuesday-Abenaa
  • Wednesday-Akua
  • Thusrday-Yaa
  • Friday- Afua
  • Saturday-Ama
  • Sunday- Akosua
